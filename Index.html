About Us

St. Gerard’s Hospital is a holistic Healthcare provider with an accredited family Medicine and Basic Nursing Training Program. Our size allows us the opportunity to be responsive and innovative and to provide very personalized care utilizing a team approach. While we have state-of-the-art facilities and technology, it is the dedication and experience of our staff and physicians that is the secret to our success. We realize that being in the hospital may be an uneasy experience. Therefore, through our values of Compassion, integrity, Respect, Teamwork, Commitment, Innovation, and Cooperation, we strive to make your visit as pleasant, comfortable and successful as possible. These values drive our organization.
